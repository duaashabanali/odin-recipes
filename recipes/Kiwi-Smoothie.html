Kiwi Smoothie

Description

This 4-ingredient kiwi smoothie is sweetened with a frozen banana to eliminate the use of ice. Itâ€™s also packed with protein, and the combination of spinach and soy milk will keep you feeling full through the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 kiwi, peeled
  • 1 banana, frozen, broken into chunks
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup soy milk

Steps

  1. Place kiwi, banana, spinach, and soy milk in the jar of a blender; cover and blend until smooth, about 1 minute.
